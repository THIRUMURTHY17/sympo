Frequently Asked Questions

What is MECHFORGE2K25?

MECHFORGE2K25 is a two-day technical and cultural symposium organized by the Mechanical Department of Vel Tech High Tech, featuring paper presentations, technical competitions, cultural events, and workshops.

Who can participate in MECHFORGE2K25?

Students from various colleges and universities are welcome to participate in both technical and cultural events.

What is the registration fee?

The registration fee is ₹250 per participant, which covers entry to all technical and cultural events and includes lunch on both days.

Is accommodation provided for outstation participants?

Yes, free accommodation is provided for outstation participants with prior intimation. Please contact the organizers for more details.

How can I register for the event?

Registration can be done both online and offline. Please refer to the "Register" section for more details and a link to the registration portal (if available).
